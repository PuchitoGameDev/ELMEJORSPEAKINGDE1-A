When Music Meets Image

Queen – A Night at the Opera (1975)

The Cover Story

It’s not a photo. It’s Queen’s crest, designed by Freddie Mercury himself. The symbols represent the band members’ zodiac signs (Lions for Leo, Crabs for Cancer, Fairies for Virgo), and on top, a phoenix symbolizing rebirth and power.

"I don’t care, dear. We’re releasing it."
— Freddie Mercury on Bohemian Rhapsody

Freddie wanted Queen to look timeless, dramatic, almost royal. This matches "Bohemian Rhapsody" perfectly: musical chaos, no chorus, constantly changing styles... but completely intentional.
